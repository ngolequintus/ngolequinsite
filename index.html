Welcome to Metaspoilt Security.
Unveiling Vulnerabilities, Fortifying Your Company.

We are your trusted partner in digital offense and defense. We offer comprehensive services covering all aspects of penetration testing and cybersecurity, designed to protect your valuable assets. Let's build a more secure digital future together.

Penetration Testing

Penetration Testing: Systematically simulating cyberattacks on a computer system, network, or web application to find exploitable vulnerabilities. The goal is to identify weaknesses that malicious attackers could leverage, allowing an organization to fix them before a real breach occurs.

Incident Response

Incident Response: A structured approach to managing the aftermath of a cybersecurity breach or attack. It involves identifying, containing, eradicating, recovering from, and learning from security incidents to minimize damage and prevent future occurrences.

Vulnerability Management

Vulnerability Management: Identifying, assessing, and remediating security weaknesses in systems, networks, and applications..

Cloud Security

Cloud Security: Protecting data, applications, and infrastructure within cloud environments.

Endpoint Detection and Response

Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR): Monitoring and collecting data from end-user devices (endpoints) to detect and investigate suspicious activities.

Data Securitys

Data Security / Data Loss Prevention (DLP): Safeguarding sensitive information from unauthorized access, use, or exfiltration.

Security Consulting

Security Consulting: Providing expert advice on security strategy, architecture, policy development, and compliance..

Identity and Access Managementr

Identity and Access Management (IAM): Managing user identities and controlling their access to digital resources.

Security Awareness Training

Security Awareness Training: Educating employees on cybersecurity best practices to reduce human-related risks..

Digital Forensics

Digital Forensics: Investigating cyberattacks and data breaches to identify the cause, scope, and perpetrators.

GRC

GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance): Helping organizations establish frameworks, assess risks, and ensure adherence to industry regulations and standards.

Network Security

Network Security: Protecting the integrity, confidentiality, and accessibility of computer networks and data using firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, etc..
